About Us

Developing Metacognitive Learners

David's Well Learners Residence is dedicated to creating an environment where students don't just learn content, but learn how to learn. We focus on developing metacognitive skills that enable students to plan, monitor, and evaluate their own thinking and learning processes.

Our residency program provides a supportive community where learners from various backgrounds come together to enhance their academic performance while developing critical thinking and self-regulation skills that will serve them throughout their educational journey and beyond.

We believe that when students understand their own cognitive processes, they become empowered, independent learners capable of achieving excellence in any field they choose to pursue.
